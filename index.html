NETFLIX IS

It is an American entertainment company founded by "Reed Hastings and Mark Randolph" on August 29, 1997, in Scotts Valley, California. It specializes in providing live broadcasting, video on demand and courier delivery of CDs. In 2013, Netflix expanded into producing movies and TV shows, and distributing video over the Internet. As of 2017, Netflix is ​​headquartered in Los Gatos, California.

In 2013, Netflix moved into the field of film and series production, and began showing the company's first original series, House of Cards. After that, the number of original programs produced by Netflix increased, including movies, series, documentaries, and stand-up comedy shows. Netflix released about 126 originals in 2016, more than any network or cable TV channel
